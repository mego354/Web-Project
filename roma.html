Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival at Rome International Airport, welcome by the trip team, transfer to the hotel, short rest followed by a tour at Piazza Navona, and a welcoming Italian dinner.

Day 2

Visit the Colosseum and Roman Forum, tour Piazza Venezia, have lunch at a local restaurant, see the Trevi Fountain, and enjoy free time to explore the city in the evening.

Day 3

Trip to the Vatican, visit St. Peter's Basilica, luxury lunch within the Vatican, and a tour of Trastevere, famous for its restaurants and cafes.

Day 4

Optional tour to Pompeii, explore local markets for gifts, return to the hotel for relaxation.

Day 5

Free time in the morning, check-out, and transfer to the airport for departure.
